The Roadmap

LexiPro launched with a focused core — 5 daily words, swipe-to-review, and a growing personal word repository. Here's what we're building next to make it the definitive vocabulary tool for professionals.

🚀 Coming Soon
🔁
Spaced Repetition

Words you marked "Still Learning" will resurface at the optimal moment. A proven algorithm — the same science behind Anki and Duolingo.

🏆
Streaks & Badges

Streak freezes, milestone badges, and your longest-ever streak record. Because consistency deserves recognition.

🔊
Premium Pronunciation

Google-quality text-to-speech for every word — hear exactly how professionals pronounce the terms you're learning, every time.

🎮
Weekly Word Games

Put your library to the test every week. Flashcard speed rounds, definition match, fill-in-the-blank, and more — learning that actually feels like play.

🌅 On the Horizon
🤖
Smarter AI Curation

Our Claude-powered word pipeline will expand coverage into niche specialisations — Patent Law, Quantum Computing, Biotech, and more.

📱
Android App

LexiPro is currently iOS-only. Android is next. The same daily vocabulary experience, available on Google Play.

📲
Home Screen Widget

Your learning words rotating on your home screen — a new word every 2 minutes, right on your lock screen or home screen. No need to open the app. Available in small, medium, and large sizes.

⌚
Apple Watch Widget

Your word of the day on your wrist. A quick glance during your commute or between meetings — vocabulary that fits your life.

✍️
Add Your Own Words

Heard a term in a meeting you didn't know? Add it to your personal library. LexiPro fetches the definition, pronunciation, and context automatically — then weaves it into your daily review.

📶
Offline Library

Your full word library available without a signal. Review on a plane, the tube, or anywhere you're off the grid — your learning never has to wait for Wi-Fi.

💡 Further Out
👥
Industry Groups

Connect with professionals in your field. See which words your peers are mastering and share vocabulary milestones.

📊
Deeper Progress Insights

Vocabulary growth charts, estimated reading level improvements, and communication confidence scores over time.

🤝
Social & Peer Connect

Follow colleagues, challenge peers to weekly word duels, and celebrate shared milestones. Turn your team's vocabulary into a competitive edge.
