Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

LexiPro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within the LexiPro mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name and email address if provided during registration.
  • Professional Profile: Your industry and seniority level to curate word lists.
  • Learning Progress: Interaction data, streaks, and word mastery status.
  • Purchase History: Managed via RevenueCat. We do not store or see your credit card information.

2. AI Disclosure & Third-Party Services

We use AI to curate vocabulary tailored to your career. We use third-party providers like RevenueCat for subscriptions and AI models for content generation. Your personal identity is never sold to third parties or used to train public AI models.

3. Account Deletion & Data Retention

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. This can be done directly in the app via Settings > Delete Account. Upon deletion, all personal data and progress are permanently removed from our active servers.

4. Subscriptions

Payments are processed through the Apple App Store. You can manage or cancel your auto-renewable subscription at any time in your Apple ID Account Settings. For more info, see our Terms (EULA).
