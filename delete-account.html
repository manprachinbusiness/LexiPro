Delete Your Account

Last Updated: April 4, 2026

You can delete your LexiPro account and all associated data at any time. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Option 1 — Delete from Inside the App (Recommended)

The quickest way to delete your account is directly within the LexiPro app:

  1. Open the LexiPro app on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Your account and all personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

Option 2 — Request Deletion by Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly:

  • Email: manprachinbusiness@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include: The email address associated with your LexiPro account

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm deletion by email.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your account credentials (email, password hash)
  • Professional profile (profession, experience level)
  • All learning progress, streaks, and word review history
  • Notification preferences and app settings

What Is Not Deleted

  • Subscription records: Transaction history is retained by RevenueCat and the relevant app store (Apple App Store / Google Play) in accordance with their policies and applicable law. We do not have the ability to delete these records on your behalf.
  • Anonymised analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage data may be retained for product improvement.

Active Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. To avoid future charges, please cancel your subscription before deleting your account:

  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → LexiPro → Cancel
  • Android: Google Play → Subscriptions → LexiPro → Cancel

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, please contact us at manprachinbusiness@gmail.com.
